My bachelor's graduation project, completed in collaboration with Si-Vision, focused on the ASIC implementation of the open-source PULPissimo RISC-V system-on-chip. The work followed a digital backend flow including synthesis, floorplanning, placement, routing, timing analysis, and physical verification using standard industrial EDA tools.
